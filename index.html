My professional life 

I am a mechanical engineering student with interest in aircraft systems. I live in Kerala, India. I am presently working on high endurance UAV for large area surveillance. This is the part of my final project.
My passion is develop aircraft system which will make life simpler. My passion for this began when I lead the first college team for the SAE Aero Design Challenge 2020. From then on I started learn more about aerospace subject and decided this is the sector I will be happy to learn and work.
My team was 1st Runner Up for Reboot Kerala Hackathon - Regional Section. We developed an application for detecting potholes and mapping them. I was also the first batch of students selected for Young Innovator Program 2018 - 2021  for my system 'Pro Drive Alerter'. I am also a certified SOLIDWORKS professional.
I believe that for having a healthy mind, we need a healthy body. I practice Maui Thai and am a good swimmer. 
I presently becoming more ready for my further studies in aerospace and to build a career in aircraft designing.
